# Twelve-Week Work Plan


## Days 1-3
- finalise topic and research question
- create repository structure
- begin literature search
- create paper matrix

## Days 4-6
- review literature and group papers by theme
- identify key papers
- begin literature review notes

## Days 7-9
- draft introduction, literature review, and research gap
- outline methodology

## Days 10-11
- write ethics, practical considerations, and planning sections
- draft non-technical summary

## Days 12-13
- revise full report
- check citations, page limits, and formatting

## Day 14
- final proofreading
- export final PDF
- prepare viva slides
